Ramen Rush (2018)

Project Info:

Made in Unreal Engine 4 using blueprint visual scripting in six weeks, Ramen Rush is a singleplayer cooking game in which the player has to make ramen for hungry customers before time runs out, hoping to make enough money to make rent for that day. I made this game for my final project during my final year of college and earned a Distinction grade for it.

My role on this project oriented around designing and programming the game, but I collaborated with a professional sound designer to implement the sound effects and music.
